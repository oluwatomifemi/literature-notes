The Handmaid's Tale

Author: Margaret Atwood

Main Characters: Offred, The Commander, Serena Joy, Moira, Luke, Nick...

Settings: Republic of Gilead, United States

Summary: The Handmaid's Tale chronicles the life of a handmaid in the republic of Gilead, an autocratic state that replaced the United States of America. This state was gorverned by a religious militant group known as the Sons of Jacob. The sons of Jacob came into power after "they shot the president and machine-gunned the congress". This group blamed the entire events on "the Islamic fanatics"...
